Our Story

Bergen's tradition bearer

Walking into Pingvinen is a bit like walking into your grandmother's living room — if your grandmother had an excellent selection of Norwegian craft beers and a penguin obsession.

We've been serving classic rustic Norwegian dishes in Vaskerelven since the doors first opened. Fish au gratin, kjøttkaker, lapskaus — all made properly, without shortcuts, from producers we trust.

From students homesick for mum's cooking to international travellers on the hunt for real Norwegian food — everyone is welcome at our tables. No dress code. No theatre. Just great food and a cold glass of something Norwegian.

Kitchen

The Menu

Classic Norwegian husmanskost, made with local produce. The menu rotates with the seasons — and one day of the week it's sacred.

Starters
Gin-Cured Reindeer
House-cured reindeer with a Nordic juniper gin, served with lingonberry chutney. Unlike anything you've tasted before.
Fan Favourite
Starters
Smoked Halibut
Delicate Norwegian halibut, cold-smoked in-house and served simply — letting the fish speak for itself.
Soup
Fish Soup
Rich, creamy Bergen fish soup — a reminder of why this city has been sustained by the sea for centuries.
Mains
Gratinert Fiskepai
Our legendary fish pie. Shredded cod baked into a golden casserole with a crispy breadcrumb crust. Served with potatoes and cabbage salad.
Must Order
Mains
Kjøttkaker
Norwegian meatballs with a deeply savoury sauce, mashed potatoes, pickled cucumber and lingonberry. Comfort food, perfected.
Mains
Plukkfisk
Shredded cod folded into creamy mashed potato, topped with crispy bacon bits. Served with flatbread and pickled onions.
Mains
Lapskaus
A hearty, slow-cooked stew of smoked lamb and sausage. Exactly the kind of thing you want on a Bergen evening.
Mains
Goat Kid Roll
Tender slow-roasted Norwegian kid goat, rolled and served with seasonal accompaniments. An adventurous house signature.
Mains
Whale Steak
A divisive delicacy — if you're curious about real Norwegian food, this is part of the conversation. Served with seasonal sides.
Every Thursday — A Bergen Tradition
Raspeballer (Potato Dumplings)
The most Norwegian thing you'll eat all week. Dense, steamed dumplings served with a trio of salted lamb, smoked sausage and bacon. We serve it every Thursday — and only Thursday.
